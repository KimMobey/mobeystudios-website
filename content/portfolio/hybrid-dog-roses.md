Wild roses have a colloquial name, They're called "dog roses". One night, at about 2am I had the sort of idea that insomnia is best at. If you look closely at this painting of dog roses, instead of beetles and bees you'll see three hybrid dogs: a Great Dane, a miniature Doberman and a Labrador.
